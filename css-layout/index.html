Data Warehousing

Data Warehousing (DW) is a process for collecting and managing data from different sources to provide meaningful business insights. Data Warehousing concepts form the basis of Business Intelligence, Data Analytics and Data Science. In this tutorial we will cover all the basic concepts of DW and touch upon the Business Intelligence tools and scenarios.
